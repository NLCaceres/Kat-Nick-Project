    Nick and Kat's Adventures!

    Our adventures all began nearly three years ago, and even though all that time has passed, every bit of it is more fun than the last!

    THIS IS HALLOWEEN

    It's almost Halloween time! And we actually haven't been spending much of that time in Disneyland... Instead we have spent it at Universal! With Halloween Horror Nights around, we have been sticking to it like glue. As of this posting, we've gone six times! Every time has been a blast and thank goodness we got the pass. Soon we shall make our return to Disneyland and enjoy all of it, but my goodness has it been a blast changing it up at Universal

    ROAD TRIPPING!

    We are about to embark an adventure unlike ever before! Neither of us quite prepared but oh so ready to go. It really is the journey we have been waiting for our whole lives. A trip to none other than the wonderful world of Disney! Disney World here we come! Through every state along the way, we couldn't possibly have a more grand of an adventure ahead. Scary, yet fun. Only two days away from the most happy place on Earth seems so close yet a huge 2500 miles away

    OPERATION: FREE THE BOOGALOOS!

    The day has come for our diabolical operation to ensue! Today is the day we free all the Boogaloos from the OC Animal Shelter or at least one of the boogaloos! :3 One particularly little Boogaloo by the name of Isabella; currently Tess, I suppose. But soon, she will be all ours! To run around at the park with, or to poop everywhere with, or to simply cuddle at home with so she can keep us warm through the cold winter months! But soon, she will be our little one forever and ever, just as we are forever and ever.
